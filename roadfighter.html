Road Fighter

An attempt at recreating a classic arcade game, "Road Fighter".

This game was made using SFML and for the partial fulfilment of our GDADPRG (Advanced Programming). It served as our introduction towards Object Oriented Programming (OOP).

Role

For this project, I worked primarily as a Programmer and Audio Manager focusing more on developing the backend of the project as well as implementing the audio for added immersion. I also looked for the animations for the models and was the one to enforce localized boundaries.

My Contributions are as follows:

  • Level Design
  • Enemy AI
  • Audio Manager
  • Player Functionality
  • Player Spin Animation
  • Player Controls
