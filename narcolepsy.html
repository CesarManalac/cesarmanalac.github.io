Narcolepsy

Narcolepsy is a game where the player must race against the clock to get their chores done before they pass out and lose the game.

Narcolepsy was one of our machine projects throughout the journey in learning game development. It follows the theme of "Home" where we had two weeks of development. It was one of our first projects using Unity.

Role

For this project, I worked primarily as the Level Designer and Audio Manager focusing more on the overall layout of the level as well as the lighting and look for the appropriate sounds for the game.

My Contributions are as follows:

  • Level Design
  • Bug Testing
  • Audio Manager
